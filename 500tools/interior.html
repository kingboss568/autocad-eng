Interior planning tools.

Companion checklists for using furniture, bathroom, lighting, and room-template CAD resources in client-facing interior workflows.

Room-readyFrom block placement to presentation notes.

Furniture Layout QA

  • Confirm circulation paths and door swings.
  • Check furniture scale against room dimensions.
  • Separate fixed millwork from movable furniture.

Finish Note Builder

  • Room name and finish code.
  • Wall, floor, ceiling, and trim finish.
  • Installation constraints or sample requirements.

Bathroom Fixture Check

  • Fixture clearances.
  • Drain and water supply coordination.
  • Mirror, lighting, and accessory heights.

Lighting Review

  • Fixture symbols match the legend.
  • Switching notes are readable.
  • Reflected ceiling plan coordinates with interior elevations.

Client Presentation Prep

  • Hide working layers.
  • Use clean lineweights.
  • Export a PDF set with one story per sheet.

Professional Note

Interior CAD resources are drafting references. Confirm site conditions, product availability, building rules, and professional requirements before construction.
