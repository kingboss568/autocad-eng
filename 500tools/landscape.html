Landscape CAD tools.

Use these English workflow aids with landscape blocks, paving patterns, site layouts, and outdoor details from the bundle.

Site-readyLayout checks for outdoor drawings.

Site Plan Intake

  • Confirm survey base, property line, and north orientation.
  • Check access, parking, and pedestrian circulation.
  • Separate existing, demolition, and proposed layers.

Paving Pattern QA

  • Verify hatch scale and joint direction.
  • Confirm pattern transitions at edges and drains.
  • Add material tags where patterns overlap.

Planting Note Prompt

  • Species or placeholder code.
  • Spacing and quantity.
  • Irrigation, soil, or maintenance note.

Outdoor Detail Review

  • Check edge restraints and drainage path.
  • Coordinate steps, ramps, and handrails.
  • Reference correct enlarged plan or section.

Export Checklist

  • Readable grayscale lineweights.
  • No missing image references.
  • Scale bars and legends appear on exported sheets.

Use Note

Landscape tools are auxiliary references. Local climate, plant selection, drainage, accessibility, and permit requirements must be reviewed separately.
