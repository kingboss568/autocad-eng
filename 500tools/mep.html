MEP coordination tools.

Practical English checklists for using electrical, lighting, bathroom, and plumbing CAD resources without losing coordination clarity.

MEP QASymbols, legends, and coordination notes.

Electrical Symbol Review

  • Match symbols to the legend.
  • Check outlet and switch heights.
  • Confirm circuits are noted where required.

Lighting Plan QA

  • Fixture tags align with the schedule.
  • Switching zones are clear.
  • Ceiling elements do not conflict with lighting placement.

Plumbing Coordination

  • Wet-wall locations are coordinated.
  • Fixture clearances are visible.
  • Drain, vent, and water supply notes are not mixed with architectural notes.

Bathroom Detail Checklist

  • Mirror, accessory, and fixture heights.
  • Waterproofing or floor slope note.
  • Tile pattern and drain location review.

Clash Notes

  • Lighting vs structural beams.
  • Plumbing chase vs millwork.
  • HVAC grille vs ceiling design.

Professional Note

MEP content is for drafting support only. Licensed engineering, code compliance, and site-specific coordination remain required.
